# Founder Pitch

## The Company

Tenio is the workflow OS for claim-status work powered by automation.

We are building the system revenue cycle teams use to run claim-status follow-up, not just another automation feature.

## The Problem

Claim-status work is operationally important but still deeply fragmented.

Teams jump between payer portals, internal systems, spreadsheets, notes, and handoffs just to answer a simple question: what is happening with this claim and what should we do next?

That fragmentation creates:

- manual work
- inconsistent interpretation
- weak ownership
- missed SLAs
- poor visibility for managers
- weak auditability

## Why Now

Automation can now do a meaningful share of retrieval and first-pass interpretation work.

But automation by itself does not solve the operational problem. Customers still need a trusted system that owns queueing, routing, review, evidence, and auditability.

That is the opening.

## Our Thesis

The winning product in this category is not just automation.

It is workflow software with automation underneath it.

Tenio is the workflow layer where claim-status work gets done:

- queue
- ownership
- routing
- review
- evidence
- audit trail
- SLA visibility

Automation makes that workflow economically and operationally viable by handling retrieval and candidate interpretation.

## Why This Becomes A Moat

If you only automate payer retrieval, you can be copied and displaced.

If you own the workflow system of record, you gain:

- operational trust
- embedded team behavior
- accumulated evidence
- audit history
- manager reporting
- downstream workflow dependence

That is where the stickiness lives.

## Go-To-Market Motion

Start with design partners that have:

- high manual portal volume
- follow-up backlog pain
- measurable SLA pressure
- willingness to centralize work in a new operating layer

Land narrow, then expand:

1. onboard active inventory
2. prove one workflow
3. add connectors and automation coverage
4. expand across queues and teams

## What We Are Not

Tenio is not:

- a generic AI agent company
- a healthcare chatbot
- a browser automation company with workflow bolted on later

We are building the operating system for claim-status work.
